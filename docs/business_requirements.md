# Business Requirements Document

## Client
DataCo Global Logistics


## Stakeholder
Chief Operations Officer (COO)
Supply Chain Management Team
Regional Operations Managers


## Project Background
DataCo Global Logistics operates a large-scale retail supply chain network across multiple countries, markets, and product categories.

Recent operational reports indicate increasing delivery delays, inconsistent regional performance, growing logistics costs, and declining customer satisfaction. Management currently lacks a centralized analytical view of supply chain performance and requires data-driven insights to support operational improvements.

To address these challenges, the Analytics Team has been tasked with conducting a comprehensive supply chain performance assessment using historical order, customer, product, shipping, and geographic data.


## Problem Statement
The organization is experiencing operational inefficiencies across various stages of the supply chain. Delivery delays, supplier performance issues, and inconsistent regional outcomes are negatively impacting customer experience and business performance.

Management requires an analytical solution capable of identifying operational bottlenecks, measuring key performance indicators, and providing actionable recommendations for supply chain optimization.


## Project Objectives
1. Measure overall supply chain performance.
2. Evaluate delivery efficiency across markets and regions.
3. Identify key drivers of delayed deliveries.
4. Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
5. Evaluate product and category performance.
6. Assess regional and market-level business performance.
7. Identify operational bottlenecks affecting service levels.
8. Provide recommendations for process improvement and operational optimization.


## Business Questions
### Supply Chain Performance
1. What is the total order volume processed by the organization?
2. What is the total revenue generated across all orders?
3. What is the total profit generated?
4. What is the average order value?
5. How has business performance varied across markets and regions?

### Delivery Performance
6. What percentage of orders were delivered on time?
7. What percentage of orders experienced delays?
8. Which regions experience the highest delivery delays?
9. Which markets have the lowest delivery performance?
10. Which shipping modes are associated with the highest delay rates?
11. Which product categories are most frequently delayed?
12. How does actual shipping time compare with scheduled shipping time?

### Customer Analysis
13. Who are the highest-value customers?
14. Which customer segments contribute the highest revenue?
15. Which customer segments generate the highest profit?
16. Which geographic regions contain the most valuable customers?
17. What is the average revenue generated per customer?

### Product Analysis
18. Which products generate the highest revenue?
19. Which products generate the highest profit?
20. Which product categories contribute the most revenue?
21. Which product categories contribute the most profit?
22. Which products are most frequently ordered?
23. Which products are associated with delivery issues?

### Regional Performance
24. Which regions generate the highest sales?
25. Which markets generate the highest profit?
26. Which countries contribute the most revenue?
27. Which regions demonstrate the strongest operational performance?
28. Which regions require operational improvement?

### Operational Optimization
29. Which operational areas contribute most significantly to delivery delays?
30. What opportunities exist to improve supply chain performance and customer satisfaction?
